Smart Tips

20% down avoids PMI. Private mortgage insurance adds $50–200/mo on smaller down payments.

Rate matters enormously. A 1% rate difference on a $400k loan is ~$240/mo.

15-year vs 30-year. 15-year loans build equity faster and save tens of thousands in interest.

Shop multiple lenders. Rates vary significantly — comparing 3+ lenders can save you thousands.
